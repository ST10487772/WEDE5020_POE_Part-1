Welcome to Sunrise Laundry Services

Founded in 2010, Sunrise Laundry Services is a trusted, community-based laundry and dry-cleaning business. We specialize in providing fast, affordable, and professional care for your clothing and fabrics.

Whether you're a local resident needing a weekly wash, a small business with linens and uniforms, or a busy professional who prefers digital convenience, we've got you covered. Our services include washing, folding, ironing, dry cleaning, and even pickup and delivery for your convenience.

Our mission is simple: “To provide fast, affordable, and professional laundry care that makes life easier for our customers.”

Join the many satisfied clients who trust us for clean clothes and peace of mind, because laundry should be the easiest part of your day.
