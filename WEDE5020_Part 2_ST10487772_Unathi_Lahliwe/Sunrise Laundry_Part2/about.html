About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sunrise Laundry Services has grown into a trusted community laundry provider, serving hundreds of satisfied customers every year. We started with a simple goal: to make laundry convenient, fast, and affordable for everyone in our community.

Mission

Our mission is clear to provide fast, reliable, and professional laundry care that makes life easier for our customers.Whether it’s washing, ironing, dry cleaning, or pickup and delivery, we take pride in every garment we handle.

Vision

We aim to become the most trusted local laundry service, offering convenient online booking, timely delivery, and exceptional customer care. Our vision is a community where laundry is never a chore.

Our Values

  • Quality: We treat every garment with care and precision.
  • Integrity: Transparent pricing and honest service are our top priorities.
  • Convenience: We strive to make laundry effortless with pickup and delivery options.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your happiness drives our business.

Why Choose Us?

Choosing Sunrise Laundry means choosing peace of mind. With years of experience, modern equipment, eco-friendly cleaning solutions, and a passionate team, we ensure your clothes look and feel their best every time.

“Fast • Affordable • Professional” That’s our promise to you.
