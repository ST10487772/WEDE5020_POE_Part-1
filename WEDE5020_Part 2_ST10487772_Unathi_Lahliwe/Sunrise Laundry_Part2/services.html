Our Services

At Sunrise Laundry Services, we provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality laundry care tailored to meet the needs of individuals and small businesses. Below is a breakdown of the services we offer:

1. Washing and Folding

We carefully wash your clothes using premium detergents and fold them neatly for easy storage. Ideal for everyday laundry needs.

2. Ironing Services

Our professional ironing service ensures your clothes are crisp, wrinkle-free, and ready to wear. We handle everything from casual wear to formal attire.

3. Dry Cleaning

Delicate or specialty garments are dry cleaned using safe, high-quality solvents. Perfect for suits, dresses, and fabrics that require special care.

4. Pickup and Delivery

Too busy to drop off your laundry? We offer convenient pickup and delivery services for homes and businesses within our service area.

5. Business Solutions

We provide custom laundry solutions for small businesses, including cleaning of uniforms, linens, towels, and other bulk items. Ideal for restaurants, salons, and clinics.
